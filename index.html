Breeds

In this world, there are over 340 breeds/species of dogs in this world. Scientists are constantly breeding different species of dogs to create different physical features. This is needed to breed dogs that fit the wants of buyers. The process of creating new breeds of dogs work, when 2 different dogs with different wanted features are bred. The features of the new dog is often similar to the ones of their parents.
